iPhone 14

The iPhone 14 boasts some of the most cutting-edge technology to date! It features a 6.7-inch super retina OLED display, an A16 Bionic chip that provides unparalleled speed, a quad-camera system that captures sharp and vibrant photos, and a battery life that can easily last two days. The phone also incorporates a holographic display feature, where you can video call your friends in 3D! Additionally, it's said to be 100% waterproof up to 50 meters, making it perfect for underwater photography

iPhone 14 Pro

The iPhone 14 Pro features a futuristic transparent OLED display that can become see-through when not in use. It's powered by Apple's A16 Bionic chip which is 70% more powerful than its predecessor. The camera system has been upgraded to include a quad-camera setup with a 108 MP primary sensor. The device also boasts of a holographic Siri interface and a battery life lasting up to 3 days on a single charge.

iPhone 14 Pro Max

The iPhone 14 Pro Max is an innovative device with groundbreaking features. It sports a 6.9-inch Super Retina XDR display, A16 Bionic chip, and a revolutionary triple-camera system with a 108MP primary sensor, a 5x optical zoom periscope lens, and a futuristic under-display selfie camera. It also boasts 8GB RAM and storage options starting from 128GB up to 1TB. With 6G connectivity and a 5000mAh battery, it's the pinnacle of smartphone technology.
