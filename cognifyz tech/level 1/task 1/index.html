AVENGERS

The Avengers are a team of fictional superheroes appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The team made its debut in "The Avengers #1" in 1963, and it was created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist/co-plotter Jack Kirby.The original Avengers were Iron Man, Ant-Man, Wasp, Thor, and Hulk. They formed the team in response to the threat of Loki, the Norse god of mischief. Over the years, many heroes have joined and left the team, making its roster ever-evolving.
